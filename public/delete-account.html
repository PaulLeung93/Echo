Delete Your Echo Account

This page explains how to delete your Echo account and the data associated with it. Echo is operated by Paul Leung.

Option 1 — Delete in the app (fastest)

Open Echo and go to Settings → Account → Delete account. You'll be asked to confirm your identity, and then your account is deleted immediately.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you can't access the app, email us from the address on your account and we'll delete it for you:

Email a deletion request

Please send it from your account email (or include your @username) so we can verify the request. We will confirm once your account is deleted.

What gets deleted

When your account is deleted, we remove:

  • Your profile (name, username, bio) and your username reservation.
  • Your Firebase authentication account (email and sign-in credentials).

Posts and comments you created may be removed or disassociated from your account. Diagnostic/crash data follows Google Firebase's standard retention periods. Requests are typically completed within 30 days.

See our Privacy Policy for more detail on the data we collect.
