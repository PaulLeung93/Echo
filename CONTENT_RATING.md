## ⚠ Related obligation this triggers (do not skip)
Because Echo hosts **user-generated content** and **user-to-user communication**,
Google Play's UGC policy expects:
- A way for users to **report / flag** objectionable content or users, and
- A way to **block** other users, and
- A stated moderation/abuse policy.

This is tracked in ROADMAP Phase 5 ("report/block — moderation — may become
required depending on Play policy for UGC apps"). **For a social app with location
sharing, treat report/block as a likely launch requirement, not a nice-to-have.**
Confirm during review whether the listing is accepted without it.
